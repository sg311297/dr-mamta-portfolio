About the Professor

Dr. Mamta Agrawal is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Bhopal. With over two decades of experience, her work bridges the gap between Applied Mathematics and Biological Sciences.

  • ✓ Principal Investigator for DST Women Scientist Scheme (WOS-A)
  • ✓ Head of PhD Panel & Fine Arts Club Coordinator
  • ✓ Mentor for PhD Scholars and Graduate Projects
